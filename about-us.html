About 7 Eight Sports

With High School schedules so strictly adhering to Group and conference assignments, it's easy to lose sight of our neighborhood. Technology may have made the world smaller, but it seems to have choked off and siloed our local sports communities. At 7EightSports we are looking to snap a whole lot of photos for you, hype the great teams, players and games, and do so while complementing the coverage that already exists. We feel quite strongly that hype begets interest, and interest begets an appetite for more coverage. We are also very interested in our local players who move on to play in college. Any athlete who has what it takes to play on any college level is worthy of some notice.

As for the local athletes who are killing it in college (or beyond), that’s something that so many of us would love to know about. As for the neighborhood, look at it in 4 pairs- Summit/Chatham, GL/NP, Westfield/SPF, Watchung Hills/Ridge. The UCC 5- Summit, NP, GL, Westfield, SPF see each other regularly, but conference ties consistently take Chatham, WH and Ridge on bus rides of 20-35 miles. The athletes in these communities grow up playing against each other throughout their youth sports days and then together again for their club teams. We feel there’s interest in what’s happening in our neighborhood. We hope you agree.
